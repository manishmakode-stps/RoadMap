What is digital forensics -> Digital Forensics is a branch of Forensics Science wich deals with identification, collection, preservation, analysis and presentation of digital evidence in legal manner.

Digital Forensice used when crime involves :
        1. Computer
        2. Mobile
        3. Networks
        4. Storage devices
It helps investigators find : 
        1. who commited the crime   
        2. Understand how the crime has happened 
        3. Recover deleted or hidden data
        4. Present evidences in court
******************************************************************

why we need computer forensics -> The need of computer forecnsics arises due to increasing 'use of digital devices in crime' and requirements to investigate and prove such crimes legally.


*******************************************************************

Understanding Computer Forensice -> Computer forensics is the process of examining computer systems and digital devices to analyze and extract data for investigative purposes.

*******************************************************************

Computer Forensice VS Other Fields -> CF is differenct from other fields like cybersecurity, data recovery, network administration, altought they are related.

cybersecurity : prevent attacks
computer forensics : investigate after attacks
data recovery : recover lost data 
network administration : manage systems
